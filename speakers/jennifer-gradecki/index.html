Jennifer Gradecki

Jennifer Gradecki was born in Milwaukee, Wisconsin in 1980 and earned a BFA in sculpture, with a second major in experimental psychology, from the University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee in 2007. She received her MFA in New Genres from UCLA in 2010 and is currently a PhD candidate in Visual Studies at SUNY Buffalo with a focus on the financialization of the art market. Gradecki's art and research emphasizes transparency and aims to make specialized knowledge and technical information more accessible.
